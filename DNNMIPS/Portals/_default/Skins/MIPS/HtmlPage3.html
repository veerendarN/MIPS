MIPS Performance Categories

Quality

60%- Replaces the Physician Quality Reporting System (PQRS)

Most Participants: Report up to 6 quality measure, including an outcome measure, for a minimum of 90 days.

Group using the web interface: Report 15 quality measures for a full year.

Group in APMs qualifing for special scoring under MIPS, such as shared savings program track 1 or the Oncology care Model: Report quality measurethrough your APM. You do not need to do anything additional for MIps quality.

Improvement Activities

15%-New Caterogy

Most participants: Attest that you completed up to 4 improvement activities for a minimum of 90 days.

Groups with fewer than 15 participants or if you are in a rural or health professional shortage area: Attest that you completed up to 2 activites for a minimum of 90 days.

participants in certified patient-centered medical homes, compareable specialty practices, or an APM designated as a Medical Home Model: You will automatically earn full credit.

Participants in certain APMs under the APM scoring standard, such as shared savings Program Track 1 or the Oncology Care Model: You will automatically recive points based on the requirements of participating in the APM. For all current APMs under the APM scoring standard, this assigned score will be full credit. For all future APMs under the APM scoring standard, the assigned score will be atleast half credit.

Advancing Care Information

25%-Replaces the Medicare EHR Incentive Prodram also know as Meaningful Use.

Fulfill the required measures for minimum of 90 days:

  • Security Risk Analysis
  • e-Prescribing
  • Provide Patient Access
  • Send Summary of Care
  • Request/Accept Summary of Care

For bonus credit you can:

  • Report Public Health and Clinical Data Registry Reporting measures
  • Use certified EHR technology to complete certain improvment activites performance category

OR

  • You may not need to submit advancing care information if these measure do not apply to you.

Resource Use

Calculated Automatically

  • Data submission is not required for the Resource Use category
  • Your Resource Use score will be automatically calculated for you based on Medicare claims history.
